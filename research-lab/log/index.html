Anderson's 8 modeling steps — legend

  1. find — identify a real situation worth modeling
  2. mathematize — choose variables, units, and equations
  3. state ideal — write down the idealized problem explicitly
  4. solve — produce a symbolic or numerical answer
  5. analyze — study limits, sensitivity, dimensional checks
  6. verify — compare to physical or external data
  7. transfer — apply the method to a new situation
  8. iterate — refine the model when it disagrees with reality
